# External Research & Intelligence References

This document tracks foundational external research and industry benchmarks that inform the AntigravitySpace and Olympus fleet architecture.

## 1. Agent Architecture & Efficiency

### [Toward Efficient Agents: A Survey of Memory, Tool learning, and Planning](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.14192)
- **Source:** ArXiv (2026-01)
- **Core Insights:** Investigates efficiency in LLM-based agentic systems, focusing on three core components: memory, tool learning, and planning. Reviews recent approaches to improve efficiency, categorizes existing works, and discusses future directions for designing and evaluating efficient agents.
- **Relevance:** Direct alignment with Olympus's Memory, Tool-Architect, and Planning layers.

## 2. Synthetic Environments & Training

### [Agent World Model: Infinity Synthetic Environments for Agentic Reinforcement Learning](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.10090)
- **Source:** ArXiv (2026-02)
- **Core Insights:** Introduces Agent World Model (AWM), a fully synthetic environment generation pipeline that scales to 1,000 diverse, code-driven, and database-backed environments for training tool-use agents.
- **Relevance:** Informs the 70000-Environmental-Harness and Enablement Labs' strategy for agent testing.

## 3. Autonomous Engineering Paradigms

### [Harness Engineering: Leveraging Codex in an Agent-First World](https://openai.com/index/harness-engineering/)
- **Source:** OpenAI
- **Core Insights:** OpenAI's experiment in building software with zero lines of manually-written code. Highlights redefining the engineer's role to focus on systems and feedback loops, legibility for agents, and architectural consistency.
- **Relevance:** Benchmarks the current Olympus "0-manual-line" goal and Sovereign Mesh maintenance.
